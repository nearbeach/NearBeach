Task

Create Task

  1. In the navigation menu at the top of the page. Click on the option “New Object”

  2. In the dropdown, click on the option “New Task”

  3. The “New Task” page will load. Fill out the form appropriately and click on the “Create New Task”

Search Task

  1. In the navigation menu at the top of the page. Click on the option “Find Objects”

  2. In the dropdown, click on the option “List all Objects”

  3. The “Search” page will load. Use the search field to search for your required task.

Note

Spaces are considered wildcards in this search.

Update Task

  1. Use the method above to find the appropriate Task you wish to edit.

  2. Edit the Task, and once completed click on the “Update Task” button
